Our Purpose

At Auto 2000 we understand how much our customers Porsches mean to them so we treat each customer and car with the highest regard.

Our experienced team of technicians make sure all cars are diagnosed , repaired and serviced correctly and efficiently so they can be enjoying their cars on the open road as soon as possible.

Whether it's a classic Porsche 928 S, or the latest 911 Carrera GTS, we can help you get the very best performance from your car.
